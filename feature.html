Features

Table Management

Create Tables for your restaurants. Manage reservations & booking for each table. Allow customers to create their own orders by scanning dedicated QR codes of each Table. Get current status of each table along with running order information associated to it.

Order Management

Book Orders using Waiterâ€™s Application, Customer Mobile using web or Mobile Application. Instant KOT generation for items included in Order. Handle takeaway orders using Cashier, Waiter & Customer Application.

KOT Management

Configure items which involve KOT process, generate KOT instantly and print them on the go to notify Kitchens. Generate specific KOTs for each Kitchens. Manage KOT operations by handling cancellation process using hierarchal approval structure. Notify Managers in case of Cancellations of KOTs.

Cashier, Billing & Invoicing

Payment handling for Billing & Invoicing process. Manage billing in case of cancellation of items or adding items for an Order. Integration with printing machines to generate Bills & Invoices.

Inventory Management

Record Inventory Items purchased with price and quantity. Dynamic calculation for current inventory standing average cost prices in order to get accurate pricing of Dishes. Create Suppliers for each inventory category and push alerts & notifications in case of stock reaches reorder level.

Reporting

Flare provides dynamic reporting like Dish Charts which at any given point of time based on the inventory level system will provide a count of each dishes which can be cooked and served, balance Sheet to match POS bills with current inventory stock levels and many more.

Running Inventory Management

Procure stocks from warehouse and maintain running inventory of semi cooked, full cooked or processed items used for preparing menu dishes. Once the item is prepare & kept in running inventory system its cost & quantity is locked based on decrement of the ingredients involved in the respective running inventory item.

Recipe Builder

Formulate each Dish by recording each ingredient with its respective Unit of measurement. These formulas will serve as defining factor for providing crucial insights & visibility for running & warehouse inventories. Recipe Builder will also provide intelligence to the hotel owner on how much and till when to procure stocks. Get insights of inventory levels for peak hours for better planning & execution.

Menu Manager

Create Menus instantly by adding Items involved in each serving. Menu Dish pricing can be configured based on factors like food costing, serving sizes ches etc. Each Menu Dish can be created with variances and combination of one or more dishes can be one Menu dish.

Left Over Management

The whole purpose of left-over management is to give the indicator of the quantity of Running inventory items left and non-settled KOTs. This indicator counts will be recorded based on its settlements and accordingly profits will be calculated.

Human Resource Management

Flare helps management team of any restaurant to handle all activities related to human resource. Employee documentation, Attendance, Payroll, Passport & National documentation expiry notifications
